syllabus:
  - title: "Analysis 1 Fall 2022 - Indicative syllabus"
    topics:
      - "Real Numbers: The archimedean property, density of Q in R, completeness axiom, Sup and Inf, decimal expansions, countability and uncountability."
      - "Real and complex sequences: Convergence and divergence, sandwich test, subsequences, monotonic sequences, limsup and liminf, Bolzano-Weierstrass Theorem, Cauchy sequences, general principle of convergence."
      - "Real and complex series: Convergent and absolutely convergent series, comparison test for non-negative series and absolutely convergent series, alternating series test, rearranging absolutely convergent series, radius of convergence of power series, exponential series."
  - title: "Calculus and Applications Spring 2023 - Indicative syllabus"
    topics:
      - "Fourier Transforms: Exponential, cosine, and sine transforms; elementary properties; convolution theorem; energy theorem."
      - "Ordinary Differential Equations: Introduction, solutions for first and second order ODEs, linear ODEs, separable, homogeneous, linear equations; special cases; linear higher-order equations with constant coefficients; systems of constant-coefficient linear ODEs; qualitative analysis of linear and nonlinear ODEs, phase plane analysis, stability, bifurcation analysis; examples from Newtonian dynamics."
      - "Introduction to Multivariable Calculus: General properties of functions of several variables, partial and total derivatives, second-order derivatives, condition for equality of mixed partial derivatives, Taylor expansions, chain rule, change of variables including planar polar coordinates."
